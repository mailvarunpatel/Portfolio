About

Building at the intersection of technology & strategy.

I've spent my career in connectivity and technology — from deploying carrier-grade networks across Africa to leading product strategy for global IoT, satellite, and cloud platforms. What drives me is translating hard engineering realities into clear product visions that teams can rally behind.

At emnify, I own product strategy and roadmap for satellite connectivity, MVNO/MVNE PaaS, and eSIM enablement — presenting directly to C-level stakeholders. I've enabled MNOs onto the platform contributing over €1M in ARR, launched 5G NSA capabilities, and brought satellite IoT connectivity to Brazil — opening deployments in previously unreachable environments.

Before that, I joined Willowmore as the first product employee and co-invented a patented IoT smart padlock. I took the product from concept through pilots across six countries to the company's first enterprise sale — a seven-figure deal with no prior customer references.

I'm now looking to step into a Director of Product role where I can lead and grow PM teams, set product strategy at a broader organizational level, and build platforms that create lasting competitive advantage.
